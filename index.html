Description of Horde


The Horde is one of the two largest political factions of the races of Azeroth, in counterpart to the Alliance. It is a group of survivors who have overcome all kinds of adversity, from destroyed worlds to exile, through family conflicts and relationships with uncomfortable allies. The present Horde is mostly about surviving in a land that hates them. The orcs are hated, like the Forsaken, because they were manipulated by corrupt forces that channeled their aggression towards unsuspecting enemies, leaving a legacy of brutality and domination. In fact, many races of the Horde have unscrupulous history of existence. Although redeemed, they are not forgiven by many of the Alliance members, who believe they have not changed.The troll and tauren, among others, are the only allies who understand them and who do not reproach them for their past actions. Ironically, all the leaders of the Horde have collaborated at some point in their lives with the Alliance and even some of them, like Sylvanas, have belonged in life.
